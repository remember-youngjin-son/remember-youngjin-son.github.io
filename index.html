Biography

Youngjin Son devoted his life to serving society as a doctor of traditional Korean medicine.
He was born in 1963 in Oksan-myeon, Uiseong-gun, Gyeongsangbuk-do, South Korea.
In 1982, he entered the Department of Nuclear Engineering at Seoul National University.
After graduation, he enrolled in the Department of Korean Medicine at Daegu Haany University to pursue a career in Korean medicine.
