Remembering Youngjin Son

아버지를 기억하며

Youngjin Son devoted his life to contributing to society as a traditional Korean medicine doctor.
He was born in 1963 in Oksan-myeon, Uiseong-gun, Gyeongsangbuk-do, South Korea.
In 1982, he entered the Department of Nuclear Engineering at Seoul National University.
After graduation, he enrolled in the Department of Korean Medicine at Daegu Haany University to become a Korean medicine doctor.
